Edzy Design System (source of truth)

Goal
- Clean, minimalist, premium feel: lots of whitespace, subtle borders, soft shadows, strong typography hierarchy.

Typography
- Display font: Outfit (headings)
- Body font: Cairo (body/UI)
- Headings: tight tracking, strong weight, short line-length
- Body: comfortable leading, avoid dense blocks

Color tokens (Tailwind v4 via @theme in src/index.css)
- primary: brand purple (used for primary CTA, focus states)
- secondary: emerald (used sparingly for success/checks)
- accent: amber (used sparingly for price/discount emphasis)
- neutrals: Tailwind grays for backgrounds, borders, text

Surfaces
- Page background: gray-50
- Primary surface: white with border (gray-200) and soft shadow
- Keep gradients minimal; prefer solid neutrals + subtle translucency (bg-white/80 + backdrop-blur)

Radius
- Cards: rounded-2xl to rounded-3xl
- Pills/badges: rounded-full
- Buttons: rounded-lg

Shadows
- soft: subtle elevation for cards and hero container
- lift: stronger elevation on hover only (avoid heavy drop shadows)

Components
- Buttons
  - Primary: `.btn-primary` (brand color, subtle glow)
  - Secondary: `.btn-secondary` (white surface + border, muted hover)
- Cards
  - Use `.card` for consistent padding, border, and shadow
- Badges
  - Rounded-full, small text, light tinted background (bg-*/10) + border

Layout
- Max content width: use `.container-custom`
- Hero pattern: centered premium card, text centered, image anchored at bottom with overflow-hidden

Do / Don’t
- Do: border + whitespace + typographic hierarchy
- Do: keep color accents to CTAs, prices, tiny highlights
- Don’t: multiple loud gradients, heavy shadows, over-saturated sections
